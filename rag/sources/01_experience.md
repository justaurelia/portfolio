**Role:** Founder and end-to-end product owner.

**Ownership:**

- Led customer discovery with bakery owners and pastry chefs.
- Owned product design, prioritization, and delivery.

**What I shipped:**
- AI-assisted recipe scaling and cost analysis using OpenAI APIs.
- Production planning and inventory logic.
- Multi-language SaaS features for bakery operations.

**Tech & tools:**
TypeScript, React/Next.js, AWS (Amplify, Lambda, API Gateway), Vercel, Supabase, Elasticsearch, OpenAI APIs.

**Key learnings:**
- Real user pain beats assumptions every time.
- Smaller milestones ship faster than “perfect” roadmaps.
- Building alone sharpens product judgment.
