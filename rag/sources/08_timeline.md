# Professional timeline (highlights)

## 2008 — Customer service (Sopra Banking Software)
Context: First job on payment engine and customer service projects.  
Insight: Foundations of software engineering and customer empathy.

## 2012 — Product Analyst
Context: Business analyst collaborating with an off-shore team.  
Insight: Cross-functional collaboration and analysis.

## 2014 — New product (Axway)
Context: Joined Axway to work on a new product (Decision Insight).  
Insight: Adapting in dynamic environments and learning new concepts.

## 2019 — Pre-sales world
Context: Blended technical and business skills, worked with stakeholders.  
Insight: Communicating technical ideas to business stakeholders.

## 2020 — Certified Pastry Chef
Context: Earned French Certificate of Pastry while continuing pre-sales work.  
Insight: Commitment to mastering a new craft.

## 2021 — Pastry business in the US
Context: Moved to the US, launched an online pastry shop, partnerships with coffee shop.  
Insight: Entrepreneurship and scaling production with a team.

## 2023 — From bakery to SaaS
Context: Resold bakery business to fully dedicate to Jucosa and started intensive AI/cloud training.  
Insight: Resilience and fast learning.

## 2024 — Building Jucosa
Context: Hired a frontend developer and focused on shaping and developing the product.  
Insight: Managing remote teams and refining product vision.

## 2025 — Launching Jucosa
Context: Bringing an AI-powered bakery management platform to life.  
Insight: Planning and scaling a SaaS product for production teams.
