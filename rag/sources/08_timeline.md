# Professional Timeline (Highlights)

Quick overview of my career in chronological order:
1. 2008–2012: Sopra Banking Software (first job — software/consulting)
2. 2012–2014: Product Analyst (second role — business analysis)
3. 2014–2019: Axway, new product (third role — product development)
4. 2019–2020: Axway, pre-sales (fourth role — technical sales)
5. 2020: Certified Pastry Chef (career pivot)
6. 2021–2022: Rendez-vous French Pastry (fifth role — bakery owner)
7. 2023–present: Jucosa (sixth and current role — founder & PM)

---

## Role 1 (2008–2012) — Sopra Banking Software
My first job. Worked on payment engine and customer service projects.  
Insight: Built foundations of software engineering and customer empathy.

## Role 2 (2012–2014) — Product Analyst
My second role. Business analyst collaborating with an off-shore team.  
Insight: Learned cross-functional collaboration and analysis.

## Role 3 (2014–2019) — Axway (Product)
My third role. Joined Axway to work on a new product (Decision Insight).  
Insight: Adapting in dynamic environments and learning new concepts.

## Role 4 (2019–2020) — Axway (Pre-sales)
My fourth role. Blended technical and business skills, worked with stakeholders.  
Insight: Communicating technical ideas to business stakeholders.

## Career Pivot (2020) — Certified Pastry Chef
Earned French Certificate of Pastry while continuing pre-sales work.  
Insight: Commitment to mastering a new craft.

## Role 5 (2021–2022) — Rendez-vous French Pastry
My fifth role. Moved to the US, launched an online pastry shop, partnerships with coffee shops.  
Insight: Entrepreneurship and scaling production with a team.

## Transition (2023) — From Bakery to SaaS
Resold bakery business to fully dedicate to Jucosa and started intensive AI/cloud training.  
Insight: Resilience and fast learning.

## Role 6 (2024–Present) — Building & Launching Jucosa
My sixth and current role. Hired a frontend developer and focused on shaping the product.  
Insight: Managing remote teams and refining product vision. Now launching an AI-powered bakery management platform.
